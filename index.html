About

Vanilla Perfected was born after frequent comments from my subscribers on YouTube requesting that I make a modpack consisting of Minecraft mods that I use in my videos. This project is entirely driven by what I as a player like to use in Minecraft, and has never intended to be anything more than that. If you've ever wondered where the word "Perfected" comes from, it's in reference to this pack being the "perfect" version of Minecraft for me personally!
